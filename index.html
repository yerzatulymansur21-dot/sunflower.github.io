Overview

SUNFLOWER is a sensorless solar tracking system that uses location + date + time to compute the Sun’s position and automatically orient photovoltaic panels.

Where the idea came from

Conventional trackers rely on optical sensors that are sensitive to dust, shading, and weather. SUNFLOWER replaces sensors with astronomical calculations.

Main advantages

  • No optical sensors
  • Lower maintenance
  • Stable algorithmic control
  • Higher long-term reliability
